Our Medical Services

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services with rapid response teams and state-of-the-art trauma facilities. Our emergency department is equipped to handle all medical crises with efficiency and compassion.

Available: 24 hours daily, 365 days a year

Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive diagnostic capabilities including MRI, CT scans, X-rays, ultrasound, and laboratory testing. Our advanced imaging center provides precise diagnostics for accurate treatment planning.

Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily

Surgical Services

Advanced surgical care with minimally invasive options, robotic surgery, and specialized operating theaters. Our surgical teams excel in both elective and emergency procedures.

Consultations: By appointment

Maternity Care

Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care in a warm, supportive environment. Our maternity wing features private birthing suites and a Level III neonatal intensive care unit.

Tours: Available every Tuesday & Thursday

Rehabilitation

Personalized physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy programs. Our rehabilitation center helps patients recover strength, mobility, and independence after illness or injury.

Programs: Inpatient & Outpatient options

Preventive Care

Wellness exams, health screenings, vaccinations, and lifestyle counseling to maintain optimal health. Our preventive care programs focus on early detection and health maintenance.

Focus: Long-term health and wellness

Service Details

Outpatient Services
Comprehensive medical care without requiring an overnight hospital stay. Includes consultations, minor procedures, diagnostic tests, and follow-up appointments across all specialties.
Inpatient Care
24-hour medical care for patients requiring hospitalization. Features private and semi-private rooms, specialized nursing units, and round-the-clock monitoring by our medical teams.
Pharmacy Services
On-site pharmacy with extended hours, medication counseling, and home delivery options. Our pharmacists work closely with physicians to ensure safe and effective medication management.
Mental Health Services
Confidential counseling, psychiatric evaluations, and treatment programs for emotional and psychological well-being. Includes individual therapy, group sessions, and crisis intervention.
Nutrition Counseling
Personalized dietary planning and education from certified nutritionists. Services include diabetes management, weight management, cardiac diets, and specialized nutrition for chronic conditions.
Home Health Care
Professional medical care provided in the comfort of your home. Services include nursing visits, physical therapy, wound care, and assistance with daily living activities for eligible patients.

How to Book an Appointment

  1. Contact Our Appointment Desk

    Call our central appointment line at +254-796894565 between 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM daily, or use our online booking portal available 24/7.

  2. Provide Patient Information

    Share the patient's full name, date of birth, national ID number, insurance details (if applicable), and the reason for the visit to help us direct you to the appropriate specialist.

  3. Select Department and Doctor

    Choose your preferred department and doctor based on availability. Our staff will suggest the most suitable specialist based on your medical needs if you're unsure.

  4. Confirm Date and Time

    Select a convenient date and time from available slots. You'll receive a confirmation SMS and email with your appointment details, doctor's name, and department location.

  5. Pre-Appointment Preparation

    Follow any specific instructions provided (such as fasting requirements for blood tests). Bring your ID, insurance card, previous medical records, and a list of current medications to your appointment.
